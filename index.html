Privacy Policy - Ahsaas App

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

Ahsaas ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Ahsaas, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and authentication credentials (all encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Blood type, availability status, last donation date
  • Location Data: Real-time and precise location information to help connect donors and receivers
  • Contact Information: Phone number and preferred contact methods (encrypted and NEVER shared with other users)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our in-app chat system (monitored for prohibited content)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent on the app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Connect Donors and Receivers: Match blood donors with receivers based on blood type, location, and availability
  • Location Services: Show nearby donors/receivers on the map and calculate distances
  • Communication: Facilitate chat between donors and receivers
  • Notifications: Send push notifications for blood requests, messages, and updates
  • Account Management: Create and manage user accounts and profiles
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Safety and Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure platform security
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Information Visible to Other Users

When you create a profile or blood request, the following information may be visible to other users:

  • Your name
  • Blood type
  • General location (city/area, not exact address)
  • Availability status

Important: Your contact information (phone number, email) is NEVER shared directly with other users. All contact information is encrypted and protected. Users can only communicate through our secure in-app chat system.

3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party services that help us operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud storage, and analytics
  • Google Maps: Location services and map display
  • Cloud Functions: Backend processing and notifications

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication using Firebase Authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

Your data is stored on secure Firebase Cloud Firestore servers with automated backups.

5. Location Data

Ahsaas requires access to your location to function properly. We use your location to:

  • Show your location on the map
  • Find nearby donors or receivers
  • Calculate distances
  • Send location-based notifications

You can control location permissions through your device settings. Please note that disabling location services will limit app functionality.

6. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications for:

  • New blood requests matching your blood type
  • Messages from other users
  • Request updates and confirmations
  • Emergency blood needs in your area

You can disable notifications in your device settings or app preferences.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View your profile and data stored in the app
  • Update: Modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable location services or push notifications
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data

8. Children's Privacy

Ahsaas is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

10. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies separately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using Ahsaas, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

12. Prohibited Activities and Content Moderation

12.1 Strictly Prohibited Activities

Ahsaas is a FREE humanitarian platform for blood donation coordination only. The following activities are STRICTLY PROHIBITED and will result in immediate account suspension or permanent ban:

  • Buying or Selling Blood: Any attempt to sell, purchase, or commercialize blood or blood products
  • Financial Transactions: Requesting or offering money in exchange for blood donation
  • Commercial Activities: Using the platform for any commercial or business purposes
  • Inappropriate Content: Sharing offensive, abusive, or harmful content
  • Fraud or Scams: Providing false information or attempting to defraud other users
  • Harassment: Harassing, threatening, or intimidating other users

12.2 Automated Content Monitoring

To protect our community, we implement automated monitoring of all chat messages:

  • Keyword Filtering: Messages are scanned for prohibited keywords related to buying, selling, payment, money, commercial activities, and inappropriate content
  • Real-time Detection: Prohibited messages are automatically blocked and flagged for review
  • Pattern Recognition: Suspicious behavior patterns are automatically detected
  • Content Analysis: All communications are analyzed for compliance with our terms

12.3 Enforcement Actions

Violations of our policies may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations receive a warning
  • Message Blocking: Prohibited messages are automatically prevented from being sent
  • Temporary Suspension: Account suspended for 7-30 days for repeated violations
  • Permanent Ban: Immediate and permanent account termination for serious violations (selling blood, fraud, harassment)
  • Legal Action: Serious violations may be reported to authorities

12.4 Reporting Mechanism

Users can report violations through the app. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

13. Data Encryption and Security Measures

13.1 Contact Information Protection

Your contact information is NEVER shared with other users. We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All personal contact information (phone numbers, email addresses) is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • No Direct Sharing: Users cannot see each other's phone numbers or email addresses at any time
  • Secure Chat Only: All communication must occur through our encrypted in-app chat system
  • Access Controls: Only authorized system administrators can access encrypted contact data for support purposes
  • Audit Logs: All access to user data is logged and monitored

13.2 Additional Security Features

  • Secure Authentication: Multi-factor authentication support
  • Session Management: Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Data Encryption in Transit: All data transferred uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Data Encryption at Rest: All stored data is encrypted on Firebase servers
  • Regular Security Audits: Quarterly security assessments and penetration testing
  • Vulnerability Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for security threats
